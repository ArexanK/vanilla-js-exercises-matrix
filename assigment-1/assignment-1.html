All about Nature

Socotra Trees

The Socotra tree, or Dragon's Blood Tree, is a rare, umbrella-shaped tree found only on the isolated island of Socotra, known for its red resin, which was historically valued for its medicinal and dye properties.

Pyramids

The Pyramids of Egypt, especially the Great Pyramid of Giza, are ancient wonders built as tombs for pharaohs, showcasing the extraordinary architectural skills of ancient Egyptians.

Pamukkala

Pamukkale, located in Turkey, is famous for its stunning white terraces made of travertine, formed by mineral-rich thermal waters cascading down the hillsides for thousands of years.

Mountain Roraima

Mount Roraima is a dramatic tabletop mountain in the Guiana Highlands, where its sheer cliffs and mist-shrouded plateau inspire myths, with ecosystems so unique they seem otherworldly.
